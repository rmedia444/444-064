Master Watercolor Painting Techniques

Watercolor painting offers a beautiful, fluid medium for artistic expression. Start with basic washes and gradients to understand water control. Invest in quality paper (140lb cold press) to prevent buckling. Learn color theory to mix harmonious palettes. Practice wet-on-wet and wet-on-dry techniques for different effects. Build layers gradually from light to dark values. Join online challenges to stay motivated. Frame your best pieces to track progress. This portable hobby provides endless creative possibilities.
