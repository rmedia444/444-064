The Impact of Big Data

In 2010, big data was becoming a buzzword as organizations recognized the value of harnessing vast amounts of information to drive decision-making. Advances in data analytics, machine learning, and storage technologies enabled the processing and analysis of large datasets, uncovering insights that were previously unattainable. Big data applications spanned various fields, including healthcare, finance, marketing, and public policy.

The ability to analyze big data allowed for more accurate predictions, improved operational efficiency, and enhanced customer experiences. However, it also raised concerns about data privacy and security, highlighting the need for robust frameworks to protect sensitive information.
